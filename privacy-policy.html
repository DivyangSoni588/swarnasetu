Privacy Policy for Swarnasetu

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

Welcome to Swarnasetu, an application designed to help goldsmiths and jewellers manage gold loans efficiently. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Phone Number: Used for secure login and user identity via Firebase Phone Authentication.
  • User Profile Data: Name, shop name (jeweller name), customer and loan records manually entered by the user.
  • Loan Details: Gold weight, interest rate, loan dates, repayment history, and images or remarks you upload.
  • Device Information: (Optional) For crash reports, debugging, and improving user experience (collected via Firebase Crashlytics if enabled).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate and log in users using their phone numbers.
  • To store and manage gold loan records securely under your account.
  • To send WhatsApp reminders for payment or gold return using customer data (optional).
  • To analyze anonymized data to improve app performance and usability (if analytics enabled).

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage). Data is encrypted during transit and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party for commercial purposes.

We may only access or share data:

  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Swarnasetu or its users.

5. Account & Data Deletion

You can request full deletion of your account and associated data by visiting our Delete Account page.

Once verified, all customer and loan data tied to your account will be deleted from Firebase within 5 working days.

6. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as your account remains active. If you are inactive for over 12 months, we may notify you before archiving your data. You can request data export before deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

Swarnasetu is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Security Measures

We follow industry best practices to protect your data:

  • All connections are encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Firebase Authentication ensures user identity security.
  • Only you can access your loan and customer records.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Updates will be posted here with the new effective date. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance.
